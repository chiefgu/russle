Do / Do not

Do

  • Use the default lockup for 95% of cases
  • Place on a calm background, not busy photography
  • Pair with Geist Mono in product UI
  • Keep clear space equal to the dot's diameter on all sides
  • Convert text to paths in Figma before exporting to print

Do not

  • Place the wordmark over busy photography or strong patterns
  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or condense the lockup
  • Recolour the coral dot
  • Change the relative size of the dot to the wordmark
  • Use the wordmark under 16px height
