## Posting cadence

One post per week. Rotate through service / retainer / case study so the feed stays varied. Keep the captions tight. The image does the work; the caption confirms what the buyer sees.

Reuse the same post template HTML, swap the headline + subhead + price block for the week's content. Screenshot, post, done.
