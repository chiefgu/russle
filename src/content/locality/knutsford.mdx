## What we do for Knutsford businesses.

Knutsford is small, but the people who shop and eat here come from a much wider catchment. Tatton brings tourists in waves. The weekend buyer on King Street drives in from across Cheshire and South Manchester. The locals who live around the town and the surrounding villages spend confidently when something feels right. That means an independent business in Knutsford has a much bigger audience than the high street suggests, and the brand and the website are usually the first thing that audience sees.

Most of our Knutsford clients are either opening a new place or refreshing one that has been trading for years. The brief is similar. The brand needs to look like it belongs on a high street people drive 30 miles to visit. The website needs to load fast on a Saturday-morning phone, make booking or buying easy, and stay current. The Google listing needs photos and posts that are actually fresh, so the customer who searches before driving in finds something worth the trip.

That is what we do. A new brand, a new website, hosting on our own platform, and the local SEO and email that keeps the same customer coming back. We build it as one project, then we run it monthly so the things that go stale, like a Google profile or a slow page, never get the chance.

Kickoff is usually a coffee in Knutsford or at the studio in Alderley Edge.
