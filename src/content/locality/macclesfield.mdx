## What we do for Macclesfield businesses.

Macclesfield is changing. The silk mills shaped the streets and the trading pace, and the indie cafés, breweries, makers, and creative studios moving in along Mill Street and Chestergate are quietly building a different town centre on top. Treacle Market every month brings a different crowd in. Customers here are more diverse than in the postcode-money villages nearby, the spend range is wider, and the brand and the site have to read clearly to both ends of that range.

Our Macclesfield clients come to us because the gap between the business they are running and how it shows up online is too big. The work is honest. A new logo that does not try too hard. A website that loads fast on the older phone someone might be holding, takes a booking in three taps, and reads clearly without designer flourish. Local SEO that catches the category searches happening from inside the town centre, because that is where most of the trade comes from.

We build the brand and the site as one project, host it on our own platform, and run the monthly work that keeps it doing its job. Kickoff is usually a coffee on Mill Street or at the studio in Alderley Edge. Most projects are live in 4 to 6 weeks.
