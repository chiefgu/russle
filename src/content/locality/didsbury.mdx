## What we do for Didsbury businesses.

Didsbury runs on indie food, drink, beauty, and lifestyle. The strip around Burton Road and the stretch of Wilmslow Road through West Didsbury hold one of the densest concentrations of independent businesses in Greater Manchester. The buyer here is younger than in the Cheshire villages, food-aware, design-conscious, and quick to scroll past anything that looks off. The brand and the website have to read as well at thumbnail size on Instagram as they do on a homepage.

Most of our Didsbury clients are cafés, restaurants, bakeries, salons, fitness studios, or small lifestyle retailers. The brief is similar across them. The brand has to look right on a feed, a window, and a homepage at the same time. The website has to load fast on a phone, look like the photography was shot for it, and make booking or buying take three taps. The Google listing has to be alive, with current photos and posts, because the customer in this area looks you up before driving over.

We build the brand and the site together as one project, host it on our own platform, and run the monthly work that keeps it going. The studio is about twenty-five minutes away in Alderley Edge. Kickoff is usually a coffee in Didsbury or at the studio. Most projects are live in 4 to 6 weeks.
