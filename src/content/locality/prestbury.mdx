## What we do for Prestbury businesses.

Prestbury is small, but the people who live around it spend confidently. The houses in the village and the surrounding postcodes are some of the most expensive in the country, and the businesses that work here tend to run on personal recommendation more than online discovery. That changes what the brand and the website need to do.

A new customer in Prestbury usually arrives having been told by a neighbour, a friend, a stylist, or a member of their club that this is the place to go. They are not landing cold from a Google search trying to weigh up options. They are looking you up before turning the car off to confirm they are in the right place. That means the website is doing a different job. It is not selling, it is reassuring. The brand has to feel comfortable next to the rest of the customer's life, which tends to be quietly premium rather than loud. The site has to load instantly on a phone, have current photos, and make booking or buying take three taps.

That is what we do. A brand that reads as careful and not loud. A website that is fast, clean, and current. Hosting on our own platform so it stays that way. A clean Google listing so the recommended customer is met with what they expected.

Kickoff is a few minutes up the road. Most projects are live in 4 to 6 weeks.
