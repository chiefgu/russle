## What we do for Hale businesses.

Hale and Hale Barns sit inside Greater Manchester on the map but feel like a Cheshire village in spirit. Ashley Road runs through one of the densest restaurant and beauty clusters in the region, and the customer base is local, affluent, and confident. Customers here judge fast. They see the front, the menu, the website, the Google profile, and they decide.

Most of our Hale clients are restaurants, salons, fitness studios, or boutique retailers who know the neighbours are doing this well and want to keep up. The brief is similar across the categories. The brand has to be specific, well typeset, and confident. The website has to load fast on a Saturday lunchtime when half the village is on Ashley Road trying to book a table. Booking integration matters because the buyer here books, they do not phone. Photography matters because the buyer scrolls before they choose. Local SEO matters because the search results on the map app are contested and the wrong listing loses you the table.

We build the brand and the site as one project, host it on our own platform, and run the monthly work that keeps it going. The studio is around twenty minutes away in Alderley Edge. Kickoff is usually in person on Ashley Road or up the road at the studio. Most projects are live in 4 to 6 weeks.
